CTFs Walkthrough

A Cybersecurity challenge, known as a Capture the Flag (CTF), involves participants searching for and exploiting vulnerabilities on target machines to access sensitive data, or "flags".

To succeed, participants must utilise their cybersecurity expertise in different domains of Cybersecurity such as web exploitation, binary exploitation, cryptography, forensics, networking, OSINT, etc.

I am particularly interested in web exploitation and penetration testing the most.

The Ethical Hacking CTFs I have completed in the past are shown below. These challenges can be found at TryHackMe, HACKTHEBOX, PG, etc.

Writeups are uploaded in a top-down approach. The oldest writeups are uploaded first (e.g. OhSINT, Lian_Yu, SimpleCTF), and the recent ones are uploaded at last.
